# Hotel Booking Behavior Analysis  
### Identifying Booking Trends, Cancellation Drivers, and Revenue Optimization Opportunities

## 📖 Project Overview
This project analyzes hotel booking behavior to understand:

- Peak booking periods
- Factors that drive cancellations
- Revenue lost due to cancellations
- Opportunities to improve pricing and promotional strategies

The goal is to help hotel management make better decisions that improve booking stability and maximize revenue.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
The main objectives of this analysis were to:

- Identify seasonal booking trends
- Measure cancellation patterns
- Determine high-risk customer segments
- Quantify financial impact from cancellations
- Recommend pricing and promotion strategies for the next business cycle

## 🚀 Final Conclusion
The analysis shows that the hotel should focus not only on increasing booking volume, but also on improving booking quality by reducing high-risk cancellations.

By aligning pricing, promotion, and cancellation policies with customer behavior, the business can improve revenue stability in future booking cycles.
